UNTW Innovation Workshop: Eradicate loneliness!

Make a difference

The point of this project is to actually implement it in real life. This way you learn best, and the most impact will happen.

Learn from experts

Learning by doing is the best, but it will get even better with experts helping out during the workshops.

Grow your network

Make friends, and grow your network with quality by working on something important with like-minded people.

Why Join?

Society is not talking about it, few does anything. 1 out of 3 feel lonely, and you now have the chance to implement a solution. We are working with YSI and the academic institutions in Oslo to arrange these low threshold workshops. They are for anyone, if you want to focus on political, social, scientifical, or technical aspects - innovation happens everywhere.
